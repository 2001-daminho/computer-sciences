The Vision Of The Faculty

To be the leading Department in Research and Teaching towards the economic and developmental growth of the nation through ICT.

  • Provide high-quality education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical mindset necessary to excel in the rapidly evolving field of computer science.
  • To be a globally recognized leader in computer science education, research, and innovation, driving advancements that positively impact society and shape the future of technology .

The Mission Of The Faculty

To empower individuals to harness the transformative power of computer science, driving innovation and shaping the digital landscape of tomorrow.

  • Train students the necessary computing knowledge and skills required to provide effective and timeless computer service.
  • Conduct research to advance the state of the art in computer science and integrate its innovations into other disciplines.
  • Train students with a solid background in computer science and knowledge that are required for post graduate studies and research.
  • Offer a comprehensive and rigorous curriculum that prepares students for diverse career paths in computer science.

The Philosophy Of The Faculty

The philosophy of Chrisland University is to provide a first class academic and research environment where the needs of the society and mankind in general are paramount to the achievements of the University.
Create an atmosphere of academic freedom for deep exploration of mind and matter as well as the expansion of the frontiers of knowledge for proffering solutions to life’s ultimate issues both for the individual and the society, and for advancing the cause of human happiness (emotional well-being), good health, environmental awareness, wealth creation and spiritual growth

A Brief History Of The Faculty

The University at inception in 2015 created two colleges which are; College of Management and Social Sciences (COMASS) and College of Natural and Applied Sciences (CNAS). CNAS started with four departments. Software Engineering programme was an arm of the Department of Physical Sciences established in 2015. During these years, Software Engineering programme under the Department of Computer Science has evolved to become a unique academic division in the production of highly trained students in the field of Computer Science.
