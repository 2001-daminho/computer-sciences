Our Story

The University at inception in 2015 created two colleges which are; College of Management and Social Sciences (COMASS) and College of Natural and Applied Sciences (CONAS). CONAS started with four departments. Computer Science programme was an arm of the Department of Physical Sciences established in 2015. During these years, the Department of Computer Science has evolved to become a unique academic division in the production of highly trained students in the field of Computer Science. In addition, the Department offers programme leading to the award of Bachelors of Computer Science degree in Computer Science.

The department has enjoyed some increment in student population though this is a major goal at this point. Lectures are delivered to the students in the assigned lecture halls on campus. The structure of the program meets the National Universities Commission (NUC)’s Benchmark for Minimum Academic Standards. It also complies with global requirements for a degree in Computer Science.

BRIEF HISTORY OF THE PROGRAMME/DISCIPLINE

The department, after verification approval at the beginning of 2017/2018 academic session, runs three programmes - Computer Science, Cyber security and Software Engineering. The department began accepting new students into the program at the beginning of the 2017/2018 academic session. The programme started with one (1) student in 2015/2016 academic session and currently, there are eighty-six (86) students in Computer Science Programme.

About The HOD

Isaac A. Odun-Ayo was born in Ilesha, Nigeria in 1962. He received the B.S, M.S and Ph.D degrees in Computer Science from the University of Benin, Benin City, Nigeria. Between 2010 and 2013 he was a faculty and Director Information and Communication Technology at the National Defence College, Abuja, Nigeria. He joined the faculty of Covenant University, Ota, Nigeria as a Senior Lecturer in October 2016 to November 2021. He is the author of one book and more than 30 articles. His research interest include cloud computing, human resource management, e-governance and software engineering. He is presently a Professor of Computer Science at the Department of Computer Sciences, Chrisland University, Abeokuta. Prof. Odun-Ayo is a recipient of the National Productivity Order of Merit Award, Nigeria for his contribution to computing. He is a member of the Nigeria Computer Society (NCS), Computer Professionals of Nigeria (CPN), International Association of Engineers (IAENG), Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) and Member Information Science Institute (ISI). He is also on the Editorial Board Member of the Science Research Association (SCIREA) Journal of Computer.
